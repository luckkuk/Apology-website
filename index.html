Dear INDU,...

I am truly sorry for my actions...

I hope ye message aap tak pahunch jaaye, chahe aap abhi kisi bhi phase ya uncertainty mein ho. Maine hamare baare mein, hamari journey ke baare mein aur apni galtiyon ke baare mein bahut socha hai. Main dil se aapse maafi maangna chahta hoon us har dard ke liye jo meri wajah se aapko mila.

Hamari journey, hamari yaadein aur saath bitaya hua har pal mere dil mein hamesha ek khaas jagah rakhega. Hamari hasi, woh chhoti chhoti baatein, aur woh khamosh lamhe bhi jo bina kuch kahe sab keh jaate the... har ek yaad mere liye bahut precious hai. Har kadam jo humne saath chal kar liya, usne mujhe aur zyada aapse pyaar karna sikhaya.

Main bas itna chahta hoon ki aap ye jaan paayein ki mera pyaar kabhi bhi kam nahi hua. Challenges aaye, misunderstandings hui, lekin meri feelings kabhi nahi badli. Hamare beech jo bond tha aur hai, woh meri zindagi ki sabse khoobsurat cheez hai. Us bond ko khona mere liye sabse bada darr hai.

Main apni har galti ki poori zimmedari leta hoon. Meri galtiyan kabhi bhi mere pyaar ki kami nahi thi, balki meri samajh aur decisions ki kami thi. Main apni un galtiyon se seekhne ke liye tayyar hoon aur jo trust toot gaya hai, use dobara earn karne ke liye jitni mehnat lagegi, main karunga.

Ye sab likhte hue main chahta hoon ki aap meri feelings ki sachchai aur mere regret ki gehraai ko mehsoos kar paayein. Aap meri zindagi ka bahut important hissa ho, aur ye sochkar hi dil toot jaata hai ki meri wajah se aapko itna dard mila. Main dil se... I'm really sorry.

Mujhe pata hai sirf words kaafi nahi hote. Lekin main sach mein jo bhi zaroori hoga, woh karne ke liye tayyar hoon taaki apni galtiyon ko theek kar sakun. Aapki khushi aur hamara rishta mere liye duniya ki sabse important cheez hai.

Bas aakhri baar itna hi kehna chahta hoon... ye sab likhte likhte main sach mein ro raha hoon. Har line ke saath sirf ek hi baat mere dil ko tod rahi hai ki jis insaan ko main is duniya mein sabse zyada pyaar karta hoon, usi ko maine apni galtiyon se sabse zyada dard diya. Is pachtawa ko shayad main kabhi bhool nahi paunga. Main dil se, poori sachchai ke saath aapse maafi maangta hoon. Agar kabhi mujhe ek aur mauka mila, to main aapka trust wapas jeetne ke liye kisi bhi had tak jaunga. Main apni poori zindagi laga dunga ye sabit karne mein ki mera pyaar saccha tha, hai aur hamesha rahega. Bas ek baar... agar aapke dil mein mere liye thodi si bhi jagah bachi ho, to mujhe dil se maaf kar dena. I'm truly, deeply sorry.

With all my love,
